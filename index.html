How does it work?

Register your business with growth

When you sign up with Growth Learning you will receive your own home page, eg. yourcompany.growth.com.au.

This is where both you and your staff will be able to login to access all the modules you have enrolled in.

Assign modules to your staff

Logging into your new home page you can enrol staff into any of the available modules. That staff member will then receive an email with an invitation to register under your business and the links to download the application.

Watch your staff grow

Your staff will fill out the modules, both theory and practical within the application.

You will se the progress they are making in real time and be able to assist in any areas that need work.
